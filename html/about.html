About us

Information about company

Mission
& Values

Our commitment is to operate responsibly
as a leading company worldwide.

Samsung is committed to complying with local laws and regulations and applying a strict global code of conduct to all employees. The company believes that ethical management is not only a tool to respond to rapid changes in the global business environment, but also a means to build trust with diverse audiences, including customers, shareholders, employees, business partners and local communities. With the goal of becoming one of the most ethical business companies in the world, Samsung continues to train employees and operate monitoring systems, while practicing transparent and fair corporate management.

Mission and Methods

Samsung follows a simple business philosophy: dedicate your talent and technology to create outstanding products
and services that contribute to a better global society than. To achieve this, Samsung places great importance on
its people and technology.

Values create the soul of
Samsung

Samsung believes that living by strong values is the key to operations good business. That's why these core values, along with the
strict code of conduct, is at the heart of every decision the company makes
go out.

Five Business Principles of
Samsung

To demonstrate its commitment to corporate social responsibility with
As a leading global company, Samsung Electronics announced
“Samsung's Five Business Principles” 2005. These principles
serves as the foundation for a global code of conduct that complies with standards
legal and ethical and implement corporate social responsibility.
